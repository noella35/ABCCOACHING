About Me

My name is Sybil Coppin-Babajide.

Sybil Coppin-Babajide, the Director of ABC Coaching & Consultancy Ltd is a good listener, very calm and engaging. She embraces equality and diversity through inclusion, allowing others to have their voices heard. She will confidently discuss whether she is the right match for an organisation or individual before accepting the coaching job. She believes to get the true benefit, we must work together in collaborative partnership where shared ownership and accountability are demonstrated

Although I am a self-starter, I was encouraged to share my talent with others. Why, you may ask? On many, many occasions and in different places, I found myself coaching friends, family and others as they shared their stories whether about their work or their lives with me. They said,

‘you are a natural, you make me feel calm, relax and you allow me to feel confident to share my stuff. You are a good listener. You really should focus on this area of your talents more as many people would benefit from it.’

I pondered this feedback, which I heard over and over again, and decided I would qualify in this area and make it official , while I continued to share my expertise. It is so fulfilling to see people faces light up when they get the ‘light-bulb’ moment and are able to progress and meet their goals.

At ABC Coaching and Consultancy Ltd, we are passionate about people development and have a strong sense of values, integrity and work ethics. We embrace openness, being appreciative of others experiences; that’s why A is about maintaining agility, B is being bold enough to have the conversation and C is having the courage to challenge. Confidence building is our foundation in people development as they are empowered to unleash their potential.

I’m passionate about people development! We focus on confidence building, unleashing potential and empowering people. We build great relationships with our clients, but not life-long attachments We help them address their un-comfortableness and they fly away, soaring high and becoming great achievers.
